Work

My field is statistics, epidemiology and public health with specific interest in genetic analysis of complex traits especially Omics‑Analysis. I am working with the SCALLOP consortium following Whitehall II, ELSA, EPIC‑Norfolk, Fenland, InterAct, NSHD and Framingham. The EPIC‑Norfolk genomewide association study contributed to consortia such as GIANT and CHARGE therefore myself as a highly cited researcher. I am a member of the Royal Statistical Society and here are my brief CV and ResearchGate profile.
