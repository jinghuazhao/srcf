About Me

I am a scientist at the Cardiovascular Epidemiology Unit at the University of Cambridge. My research spans the fields of statistical genetics, epidemiology, and public health.

My work involves large-scale genomic studies (GWAS), data integration, and developing reproducible workflows in R. I am actively involved in initiatives like SCALLOP and participate in open-source software development.

I also regularly contribute to conferences and communities such as useR!, promoting transparent and reproducible science.
